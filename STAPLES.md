# Food Staples — Allison's recurring items

**What this is:** A lookup of the foods Allison eats over and over, with her
shorthand → the full thing. So when she logs "protein shake thing" or "strawberry
bottle" or "usual smoothie," Claude knows exactly what it is and logs it clean —
she never has to re-describe a staple.

**Why it exists:** Her words (2026-06-23): _"i ate alot fo the same things so i
want you to know what these things are."_ The food-log app is a vibe log (no
macros) — this doc is the recognition layer on top of it.

**Decided:** Shorthand-first catalog. Macros only where a photo nailed them down
(not the point of the app — just handy when known).

**Built:** v1 — 2026-06-23, from her last ~30 logged meals.

**What's next:** Add items as new staples appear; lock a brand/macros when she
sends a photo (like the Müller drink). If a staple changes (e.g. half → whole
bottle), update the note here.

**Links:** `README.md` (app), `setup.sql` (schema). Live app:
https://allisonecalt-sudo.github.io/food-log/

## Car grab-and-go need (2026-06-23)

She needs a **one-hand drinkable bottle for the CAR** (cottage cheese + yogurt are
good but need a spoon → not car-friendly). Goal for a car drink: **high protein +
low sugar + LESS processed.** No single bottle nails all four. Shortlist put on her
grocery list (allison, Dairy): **Danone PRO drink** (~20g protein, low sugar, but
processed/sweeteners) · **plain kefir** (cleanest, ~9g protein) · **plain goat
yogurt drink** (clean, ~7g). Label rule at the shelf: protein ≥10g, single-digit
sugar, short ingredient list (avoid sucralose / modified starch / many stabilizers).

- **"strawberry bottle thing" / "strawberry drink"** → **מחלבת טל (Machlevet
